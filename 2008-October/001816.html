Hello All

Per http://bugs.gpodder.org/show_bug.cgi?id=178 I have now enabled
voting at http://bugs.gpodder.org

What this means is that new bugs or features will be marked as
UNCONFIRMED until there are atleast 5 votes, after which it will change
to NEW.   Each user can cast a maximum of 2 votes per bug.

This is the first time I have enabled voting on a Bugzilla installation
so as far as I can tell all is well!  If you experience any issues then
please don't hesitate to contact me at paul at rudkin.me.uk .

The vote numbers above maybe reviewed in the future if they turn out not
to be practical.

Regards
