Am 2011-04-27 15:53, schrieb neric:
> What I really want of a feed client is to keep feed entries even after
> they disappear from the feed.
> But it's not really how gpodder.net works, is it ?

Actually that's partially what gpodder.net does right now. It stores
metadata about episodes (feed-entries) and keeps them after they
disappear from the feeds. It does, however, not store the referenced
media files, but that's probably not relevant for non-podcast RSS feeds
anyway.

Apart from that gpodder.net would of course still lack the UI for being
an RSS-Reader and the existing API support is also pretty limited.
