Hi,

We've once more updated my.gpodder.org today. We now have
* subscription history
* device settings (still only one device per , but be prepared for the
next gPodder release!)
* a podcast-toplist based on the number of subscribers
* podcast-suggestios per user, based on already subscribed podcasts

Please log in at http://my.gpodder.org/, have a look at the new
features and provide us some feedback - especially on the suggestions.

If you want to help, check out http://repo.or.cz/w/mygpo.git and send
some patches.

Thanks!

-- Stefan
