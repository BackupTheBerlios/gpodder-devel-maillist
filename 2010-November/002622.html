Hi all.  I've been a happy gPodder user for about a year and a half, 
basically ever since the Amarok team dropped support for version 1.4. 
I'd say that apart from my Web browser, it's the one piece of software 
that I use most often.

I'm a techncial writer by profession, so by way of thanks to the 
developers and maintainers, I'm working through the end-user 
documentation on the wiki and hopefully bringing it up-to-date as time 
permits.  I've started with the configuration/preferences options, at:

     http://wiki.gpodder.org/wiki/User_Manual#Configuration

I'd love to hear any constructive suggestions or corrections.  (My 
username on the gPodder wiki is "RansomOttawa.")

     http://wiki.gpodder.org/wiki/List_of_Configuration_Options
