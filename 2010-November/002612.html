Hey Sam,

If it says you're missing the MTP plugins you need to check your distro's
repositories for the package that provides mtp.py or whatever it is that mtp
requires.  I just went through this a couple weeks ago when I got my Sandisk
Sansa Fuze and I came to the conclusion that it's better to use it in MSC
mode (just appears as a USB drive) and then you just tell gpodder where your
Sony mounts to.  In my case /media/Sandisk/podcasts or something like that.
Works perfectly.
