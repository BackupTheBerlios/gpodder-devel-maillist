You wrote:
> Is it just me or are the the advanced settings gone? I'm using version 2.0.

Yep, the are hidden. Use this key combination in the preferences
dialog for now:

Fullscreen-Fullscreen-Plus-Minus-Plus-Fullscreen

After heavy protests from users on talk.maemo.org, the "Advanced"
settings button is coming back in one of the next versions ;)

> Also, Just out of curiosity, is finger-scroll gone?

You can set the advanced configuration option "enable_fingerscroll"
to True, but it's not really supported anymore (makes the UI sluggish
and might even be broken right now).
