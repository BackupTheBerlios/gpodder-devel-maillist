There is attached to this email 3 mockups for the maemo platform.
The first one is the main window (gpodder1_main_window.gif)
The Open button leads you for each podcast to the second mockup
(gpodder2_episode_list.gif)
The Downloads button leads you to the third one
(gpodder3_download_list.gif).

I think that putting 2 columns in the main screen on the maemo platform
is too much, that's why I've separated the podcast list and the episode
list.
You can even imagine a smooth transition between each screen.

On the maemo platform, I think also that common operations should be
imediatly available via big buttons (play/stream/delete/listing...).
Non common operation could be reached via the top menu or via a long
press on an element.


As a side question, I don't see the updated french localisation on the
new builds on maemo platform, are the locales separated for each platforms ?
