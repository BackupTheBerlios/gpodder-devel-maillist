(gpodder3_download_list.png)

I think that putting 2 columns in the main screen on the maemo platform
is too much, that's why I've separated the podcast list and the episode
list.
You can even imagine a smooth transition between each screen.

On the maemo platform, I think also that common operations should be
imediatly available via big buttons (play/stream/delete/listing...).
Non common operation could be reached via the top menu or via a long
press on an element.
