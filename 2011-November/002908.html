Hi,

I've assembled a native application for Mac OS X 10.6 (Snow Leopard)
x86_64. It contains gPodder and its dependencies and doesn't require
anything aside from the standard system libraries.
It is not an X11 application, but uses the Gtk-quartz backend.
It's based on gPodder 2.20, with a few quick and dirty hacks
(https://github.com/elelay/gpoder-osx-bundle/blob/master/gpodder-gtk-osx-application.diff).

More info here : https://github.com/elelay/gpoder-osx-bundle

Current limitations
- the locale is forced to French for the moment.
- no html show notes, no synchronisation with an iPod, no conversion to
  mp3.


I've not tested on another computer, though,.
Could somebody try
https://github.com/downloads/elelay/gpoder-osx-bundle/gPodder.zip
and tell me of the outcome ?


Cheers,
Eric
