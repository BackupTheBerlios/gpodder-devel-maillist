> a customized folder
> 
> an recently i installed gpodder 8.9 and set dowload folder to that
> folder but it was not recognized ....
> 
> don't tell me that i have to download them again

You don't have to download them again, it's just that gPodder won't
display the 0.8.0-downloaded podcasts in its GUI. You simply have to
copy the files from your downloaded folder somewhere else where they
are easily accessible.
 
> what can i do?

If you really want your already-downloaded podcasts to be visible in
gPodder, you have to rename the files and folders with the respective
md5sums of the URLs of the channels or episodes. This means the
following:

[channelname]/[episodename].mp3
  ->
[md5-of-channel-url]/[md5-of-episode-url].mp3

> BTW the new verion is much better..

Thanks, I'm glad you like it. The naming change mentioned above is one
of the changes that makes gPodder more reliable and stable, because it
does not depend on unique-named channels and podcasts anymore.
