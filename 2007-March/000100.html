Thanks for making the changes! I just realized that the reason my
poking and prodding of the source did not result in anything, is that
gpodder currently cannot be run from within the build tree. Doing
"python setup.py build" followed by bin/gpodder results in the same

files were loaded from the build dir and the rest from the installed
version)

Would you reckon it will be a good idea to make gpodder runnable this

probably to have a "python setup.py run" target that would add the
