On Wed, Dec 30, 2009 at 03:45:15PM -0200, Silvio Sisto wrote:
> Would it be possible to pause a random running download if I happened
> to lower the number of concurrent downloads and it became lower than
> the current number of running downloads? Also start a random download
> when I raise this same number?

Starting new threads is implemented in the development version. Pausing
a random download is not for technical reasons. In the mean time, simply
pause a download of your choice after you lower the number of downloads
or wait until the excessive downloads have finished, and no new threads
should be started.

HTH.
