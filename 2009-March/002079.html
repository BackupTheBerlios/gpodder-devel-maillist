I made some changes to my podcast list on my N810 then uploaded my
list to my.gpodder.org.
Then I downloaded my list on my desktop PC and since I already had my
podcast list there, most of my podcasts were duplicated.
Since I didn't want duplicates in my podcast list I decided to erase
my episode database (~/.config/gpodder/database.sqlite) and then start
gpodder with an empty podcast list.
I was then able to download my podcast list without duplicate podcasts.
Is there a way to erase all my subscriptions from within gpodder?
I understand you could remove them one by one, but when you have near
30 podcasts it's not fun.
Maybe there should be a feature to let user select more than one
podcast and remove them.
