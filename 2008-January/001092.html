Hello!

I've just added a nice feature that I needed to gPodder's SVN trunk (the
development version), a short description and screenshot can be found on
my blog:

http://tperl.blogspot.com/2008/01/gpodder-n800-and-bluetooth.html

You can now transfer Video Podcasts to your Bluetooth devices (i.e. the
Nokia Internet Tablets) using gPodder. If used with the "tablet-encode"
script available from the mediautils package, gPodder will automatically
convert the videos to a good size for the Internet Tablets.

Enjoy,
Thomas
