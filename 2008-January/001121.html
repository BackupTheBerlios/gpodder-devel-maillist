Hello All

I just wanted to let you know that the gPodder Bugzilla database is now
on a faster server and is still accessible from same url
http://gpodder.thegithouse.com .

I have reduced the severity list to the following:-

* blocker 
* major 
* normal
* minor 
* feature request

Currently there is a priority list for P1...P5.  I personally think this
is a little misleading (for example which is the highest priority?)  I
propose that we change them to the following:

* high
* medium
* low

Let me know your thoughts and if we are all in agreement I will make the
necessary changes.

Paul
