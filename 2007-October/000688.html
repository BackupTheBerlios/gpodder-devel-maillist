On 10/16/07, Chris Arnold <carnold at electrichendrix.com> wrote:
> Hello all! I am using gpodder 0.10.0 and i wondered if it supports
> artwork? So when podcasts have artwork, it is downloaded and used with
> the podcast.
>
> Chris


 Hello Chris,

If you have a color screen iPod and have PyID3 installed (get it from
http://nikosapi.org/gpodder/) then gPodder will try to extract the
cover art from the mp3 file when you sync to the iPod. If you don't
have it installed then gPodder will transfer the channel's cover art
(the one visible in the left hand column). You can also set the
channel cover art manually by right clicking on the channel and using
Thomas' fancy drag and drop feature in the cover tab :)
