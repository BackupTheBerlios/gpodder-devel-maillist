On Sun, 2007-10-28 at 22:05 -0500, Aravind Seshadri wrote:
> Has anyone come across the problem of file name being too long. 
> 
> The guys in WSJ (Wall Street Journal) have a podcast which can be
> found
> here http://feeds.wsjonline.com/wsj/podcast_the_journal_report
> 
> I am able to download the podcasts but I cannot sync it to my iPod.
> For
> some reason WSJ has the title of the podcast as a very long string (in
> fact a paragraph of words). So when I try to sync my iPod using the FS
> based synchronization I get the following error.
> 
> ``Error opening /media/IPOD/Podcasts/The Journal Report/When companies
> form alliances with other businesses, opportunities -- and perils --
> abound. Dr. Nancy Nix, Director of the Supply and Value Chain Center
> at
> Texas Christian University tells the Wall Street Journal's George
> Anders
> what it takes to make collaboration more effective..mp3: File name too
> long.''
> 
> What can I do to get around this problem?

Try the following:

Open up the preferences window, go to the "Player" tab and in the
"Synchronization Options", check "Custom filename:" and enter
"{episode.basename}" (without the quotes). This should give you the file
names as they appear in the episode's download URL.

Does this fix your problem?
