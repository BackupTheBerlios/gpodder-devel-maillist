Hello, Aravind!

On Sat, 2007-10-27 at 20:36 -0500, Aravind Seshadri wrote:
> I am unable to add the following rss feed.
> http://rss.vanguard.com/pod/investment.xml I am able to access the
> podcast feed using Flock but gpodder returns the following message.
> 
> ``Error handling channel: The channel could not be added. Please check
> the URL or try again later''

I've looked at this issue, and after a short debugging session, I
realized that the feed you mentioned has "https://" URLs for its
episodes, and gPodder will automatically reject all URL schemes it
doesn't know about.

Simply adding "https://" as a known scheme (as our download libraries
can already handle these) should make gPodder and your feed happy again.

A new gPodder release is _very_ near, but if you don't want to wait, you
can always run gPodder from SVN to get all the neat goodies that we've
prepared in the last month:

http://gpodder.berlios.de/running-from-svn.html
