First of all, I see I am not the only one with too
high CPU load when downloading - Thomas has made a
patch to solve this issue.

My element for the wishlist: have gPodder produce a
sound when a download is comlete, like DownThemAll
has. It is very convenient when you are not watching
the download progress to get an audible indication
that the process has finished.
