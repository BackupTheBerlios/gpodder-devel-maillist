On Sun, 2007-10-28 at 17:21 +0100, Thomas Perl wrote:
> Hello!
> 
> I've just released gPodder 0.10.1, the October 2007 release of gPodder.
> Thanks to all who were involved in getting gPodder to where it is today,
> below you will find the news announcement, the changelog summary and the
> list of people who directly contributed to the changes in this release,
> I hope I didn't forget anybody :)
> 
> For the next release cycle, I hope that we will be able to fix some
> long-standing feature requests and wishlist bugs. I've looked at our
> current TODO list and picked out some items that would be nice to have
> and could probably be fit into the ~ 1 month release cycle:
> 
>  * Parallel/faster feed updates
>    (or even asynchronous/backgrounded updates?)
>  * System tray icon / libnotify integration
>  * Make iPod sync / FS-based sync two-way (delete on iPod)
> 
> It would be nice if some volunteers could come up and help implementing
> at least parts of these features, of course I can provide help as time
> permits and will try to devote some free time to gPodder again.
> 
> Anyway, Berlios' FTP server seems to be down at the moment, so I cannot
> upload the tarball to the usual location. Until this is fixed, you can
> download the tarball here:
> 
> http://khan.thpinfo.com/~thp/gpodder-0.10.1.tar.gz
> 
> After this has been fixed, gPodder 0.10.1 will be available from:
> 
> http://prdownload.berlios.de/gpodder/gpodder-0.10.1.tar.gz
> 
> Thanks for your time and I hope you'll enjoy this release :)
> 
> Thomas
> 
> ------------------------------------------------------------------
> 
> A bit more than a month since our last release, we're proud to announce
> gPodder 0.10.1. This release focuses on minor bug fixes and some
> usability improvements. For users that download many episodes at once
> (and maybe even in parallel), gPodder will not update the GUI so often,
> resulting in less CPU usage. Owners of newer iPods with video support
> can now synchronize their video podcasts with the "Video Podcasts" menu
> item on their iPod (previously, gPodder synchronized videos to the
> "Videos" menu).
> 
> Grab your copy of gPodder 0.10.1 now or wait until packages appear in
> your distribution in the next days or weeks. If you like gPodder and
> want to show your appreciation, please have a look at my wishlist or
> look at the donations page. The gPodder 0.10.1 tarball is available from
> the downloads page.
> 
> 
> Features
>       * Downloading is less CPU intensive now (GUI update rate limiting)
>       * The "Transfer" dialog hides the unnecessary second progressbar
>         now (screenshot)
>       * iPod: Video podcasts are now synchronized to the "Video
>         Podcasts" menu
>       * Support for "https://" URLs for episodes
>       * Episode list: New "Save episode to folder" menu item
>       * Episode list: Update icons after synchronization (when "played"
>         status changes)
> Code changes
>       * Code removal: Symlinking code, unneeded imports
>       * Unified code for copying files (GladeWidget.show_copy_dialog)
> Bugfixes
>       * Channel list selection/update bugfixes
>       * Compatibility updates for Win32 (experimental support)
>       * Really load channel metadata (custom title and HTTP
>         authentication data)
> Thanks
>       * Wilfred van Rooijen: Excessive CPU usage bug (bug report and
>         testing)
>       * Nicolas Quienot: Patch to sync videos to the "Video Podcasts"
>         menu (iPod)
>       * Aravind Seshadri: Bug report ("https://" scheme support)
>       * Nick (nikosapi): Patches: Channel list selection; channel
>         metadata loading
> _______________________________________________
> gpodder-devel mailing list
> gpodder-devel at lists.berlios.de
> https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/gpodder-devel
