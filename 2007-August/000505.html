With it being a filesystem-based MP3 player, you can always rsync the
contents of your MP3 palyer to your home directory, and you'll have your
whole device backed up. Subsequent rsync runs will only copy the changed
files. Something like:

rsync --recursive --archive --progress --verbose
--delete-after /media/mp3player ~/mp3-player-backup-tree/
