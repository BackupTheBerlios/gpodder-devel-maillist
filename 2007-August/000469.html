Good Alaskan Morning!

gpodder-0.9.4 is out of testing and now in F7 updates.

This package requires python-eyed3 and python-gpod to bring in some of
the optional ipod support. Due to the legal status of mp3 and the like
inside the fedora project, i can't roll in patented codec support in
package requirements, but lame and mplayer are available from 3rd
party repos such as livna.  So users who want that support can
configure livna on their systems.

I thought it best to leave vorbis-tools out of the requirements, but I
could be persuaded to add that in as well to drag in the optional ogg
encode/decoding support.

Currently, it appears that gnome-bt doesn't exist in the fedora
repository.  I'm going to look at getting that included in Fedora if I
can. If there is a fedora contributor on this list who is interested
in submitting a gnome-bt package, let me know, and I can work with you
as your package reviewer so we can streamline the inclusion process.

-jef
