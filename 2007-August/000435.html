Hello!

I just wanted to inform you that Pierre-Luc Beaudoin[1] has added
gPodder support to his Sofa Media Center[2]. Sofa reads in gPodder's
download directory and presents it in its media center interface. Maybe
you want to give Sofa a try, I haven't tried it out myself yet, but the
project looks promising, and surely is interesting for gPodder users.

[1] pierre-luc at squidy.info
[2] http://sofa.sf.net

Because of this integration, I have been thinking about restructuring
the gPodder codebase a bit to be usable as library as well, so
developers can access a kind of gPodder API to use gPodder's
functionality from their programs (i.e. media centers, etc..). Any
thoughts?


Enjoy,
