Am 2011-01-21 12:16, schrieb Stefan Kögl:
> Currently gpodder.net doesn't have an API endpoint to retrieve a list of
> episodes for your podcasts. 

I've to correct myself here, because you can use the endpoint
"Retrieving Updates for a given Device " [1] to get new episodes for the
subscribed podcasts. Just use timestamp=0 the first time, and afterwards
the timestamp from the last response.

Of course you can also use mygpo-feedservice as mentioned in my previous
mail... just pick what is more suitable for you.
