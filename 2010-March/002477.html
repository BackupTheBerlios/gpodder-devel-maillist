Hello,

I attached a quick mock-up of the device/playlist feature to this mail.
Alternative you could open this link:
http://www.imagebanana.com/view/r5ajv14/gPodder_mockup.png

If someone selects the device in the list it could be possible to show
some general information about the device or maybe it could be a good
idea to show the device settings in the right pane.

If someone selects a playlist stored on the device (look at my mock-up
for this use case) it would be nice to integrate the current "Manage
device playlist" window into the right pane.
This is useful for file-based media players. I don't really sure which
options are possible for a MTP devices or iPods.

What do you think about it?

~ Bernd
