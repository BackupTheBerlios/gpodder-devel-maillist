I've noticed a few usability flaws.

- No hability to unsubscribe to several or all podcasts.
- No hability to select all/none in the "Download podcasts" popup
dialog in maemo.
- No hability to pause/unpause all downloads in maemo. You can
manually select podcasts and pause them though. Maybe add a
"pause/continue all" button at the bottom?

clear your selection meaning you can't pause or do anything to a
single download anymore (this is only for maemo).

Comments? Should I post them as bugs?
