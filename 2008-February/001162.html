> For those wondering why to change the position of the entryAddChannel,
> besides IMO being more intuitive and clean, as a very general guideline, in
> left-to-right languages, we should avoid having a selection in one control
> change a control above it or to its left.
> 
> Here is the patch:
> http://www.cypherbios.org/files/gpodder_viewtoolbar_addfeedposition.patch
> 
> So, to sum up the contents of this little patch:
> * Add a menu View > Toolbar (checkbox item to show hide the toolbar);
> * Change the position of entryAddChannel;
> * Add a "Update all" button into the bottom of the channels list;
> * Remove the "Update Feeds" from the toolbar.
> 
> These changes can be seen in this screenshot:
> http://img209.imageshack.us/img209/7018/gpodderviewmenuej3.png
