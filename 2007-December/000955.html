Hi all,

As reported on the Rockbox fora, the newest Apple
players are completely different hardware from the
previous iPods, and apparently there is also some
hardware encryption making it impossible for the
rockbox developers to make a port to the new ipods. I
would expect that these difficulties apply to all open
source projects trying to communicate with the newest
iPods.

Regards,
Wilfred
