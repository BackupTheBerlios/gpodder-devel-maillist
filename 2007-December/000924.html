Hello, Chris!

Chris Arnold wrote:
> Is there a way to delete the channels in gpodder? Is it possible that
> something has become corrupt? Just looking for some way to get this working.

gPodder stores all its settings in ~/.config/gpodder. If you quit
gPodder, remove this folder (and probably also remove
~/gpodder-downloads/ or wherever you store your downloads) and re-start
gPodder, it will start out with a new, fresh config.

I've also added this as a FAQ entry now.
