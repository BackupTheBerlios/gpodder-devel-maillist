Hello, Chris!

Chris Arnold wrote:
> Using 0.10.3 and trying to get a podcast added to gpodder. This podcast
> is located here:
> http://www.insight.org/site/PageServer?pagename=Insightspodcasting
> and when i click it (i get asked to sign-up for some newsleter, i click
> "no thanks, take me to the podcast) then firefox gives a "i don't
> understand the itms" error. I understand 0.10.3 supports itms and some
> other new protocols. How do i add this podcast to gpodder? I have copied
> the link and pasted it into gpodder and gpodder gives a "not a podcast
> link" message.

The URL you would copy is
"http://ax.phobos.apple.com.edgesuite.net/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/browserRedirect?url=itms%253A%252F%252Fax.phobos.apple.com.edgesuite.net%252FWebObjects%252FMZStore.woa%252Fwa%252FviewPodcast%253Fid%253D89603501%2526amp%253Bs%253D143441"
and gPodder resolves this URL to:

http://feeds.feedburner.com/InsightForLivingDailyBroadcast

It could happen that your feed DB gets corrupted. In this case, just
delete all .db files in ~/.config/gpodder/ and gPodder will re-create
them on next startup.
