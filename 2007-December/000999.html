Hi,

I'll get to your questions in a bit, but first I want to report something
really annoying. You see, my iPod has the tendency to reset some of my
podcast play statuses at what seems random times. (I'm sure that there's a
trigger for this, though.)

It appears that the play counts of these podcasts hadn't been raised to 1.
After some further investigation, I discovered that *all* play counts on my
iPod are messed up (I might check in with the folks at gtkpod for this
later). Some tracks were supposedly played like 85 times - which is
impossible - but most of them have 0 in the play count field! There's not
much you can do about that, considering that it's probably a
gtkpod/libgpod-related issue. However, you might be able to get the podcast
play statuses working correctly by making gPodder check the play status of a
podcast to set the play count accordingly (i.e. if a podcast has been
played, make sure that its play count is set to 1 and not 0). Is that
possible?
