Hello!

I've just moved our list of feature requests (the TODO list) from our
SVN repository (where I deleted it) into the gPodder Wiki. This should
make it easier to bring in new ideas and features and is also a good
place for new people to see the features that are easy to implement:

http://gpodderwiki.jottit.com/todo

automatically delete old, unplayed podcast episodes on startup.
