OK, so I *specifically* chose the wrong channel to try
the cover feature. Speaking of luck.

I live in the States but I am originally from The
Netherlands (as you've guessed correctly in your
mail). The radio landscape here is a barren landscape.
All commercial radio stations are crap, too highly
formatted. It's like McDonalds: you know *exactly*
what you're going to get when tuning in, but it's all
low quality.

Podcasting seems to be very popular here, but the big
drawback is that it is very difficult to judge what is
good and what is bad. Also the technical side I don't
like. Most podcasts are 96 kbps or lower, so although
I have a super-duper internet connection, high-tech
iPod, I can surf around the world in 80 milliseconds,
I am still listening to something that sounds like an
AM-radio or worse.

Anyway. I'll try the pacific coast once - see what
happens :-)).
