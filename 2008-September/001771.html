On the Maemo version: Tap on the episode (in the "Podcasts" tab), and
you will get a menu that has items "Download", "Do not download",
"Episode details", etc.. Click on "Do not download" and the episode
will not be marked as "new" and not appear in the "New episodes
available" dialog.

This works (just confirmed) in the 0.12.3 version of gPodder, available
in the Maemo Extras repositories for Chinook and Diablo.

Hope this help.
