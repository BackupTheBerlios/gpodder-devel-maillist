Hi, gPodder developers. I'm the person who created the new gPodder icon.

The 'antenna' is actually the official Podcast symbol, and a great way
to tell users that "This is a podcast application". I could probably
make the waves around it stronger to make its apperance stronger. That
way it would also not look that much like a web browser as some of you
think that the current version does.

As for having the green man as part of the current icon I'm afraid
that's not ideal at all. Again, I -really- understand that the green man
means a lot to you developers and power users that participate on this
list, but new users will likely be more confused than helped from it. As
I've said before, it would really be best to keep it in the about dialog
(look how gedit does this) and on the website.

-Kalle
