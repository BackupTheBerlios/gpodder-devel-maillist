To answer Bernd's question, for the time being I listen to the
majority of my podcasts at my PC and when I'm about to go somewhere I
transfer an episode or two to my n810 via the bluetooth sync feature.

When I do happen to use my rockbox player I also use subdirectories
for each channel like Wilfred, but I use rockbox's bookmarking feature
to keep track of what I've listened to and where I am in the file.
