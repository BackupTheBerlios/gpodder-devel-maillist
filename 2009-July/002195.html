0.17.0 is built in rawhide and in Fedora 11 updatest-testing.  As soon
as the next update push happens it should be available.  After about a
week, barring any bug reports the updates-testing package will be
moved out to stable updates.

And FYI, 0.17,0 will not work on Fedora 10 because of the minimum gtk2
requirement of 2.16 is not met on F10.
