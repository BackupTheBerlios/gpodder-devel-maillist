I suspect that you are referring to the account with the email address
sisto.silvio at gmail.com. Actually I can see only one device (with the
Device ID Nokia-N810-43-7) configured for this account (the legacy
device has been deleted). However, you should configure both devices
(gPodder on PC and N900) as devicerent devices in the webservice. You
can switch synchronization on via the webservice. Checking for new
Episodes in gPodder should then show a dialog to accept your
sync-changes.
