They are settings for the type of USB support. The 2 types are MSC and MTP.  
MSC makes your device look like a mass storage device and you can copy your  
files directly into a folder on the device. I'm not sure how MTP works,  
because I've never gotten it to work correctly.

On my device the the Auto setting let the device switch between MSC and MTP  
and that is when I had trouble. The device would show up as a mass storage  
device, but gPodder would do exactly what you are describing. After I  
switched it to MSC mode the problem went away.
