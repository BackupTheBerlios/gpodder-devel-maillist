Thanks, I've added the translation to the Git development version :)

Counting the .po files, we now have 12 translations, which means that
gPodder is available in 13 languages (=12 translations + English). I'll
hope to support our translators a bit more in the future and coordinate
the effort to getting up-to-date translations and an easier workflow for
updating and submitting translations.

Also, I know that some of the strings in gPodder are not very clear or
could be formulated in a better way. If you find these during
translation
or during use, please point them out, so we can fix them. Even if this
means that the translators have to re-translate the string, users will
have an easier time to use and understand gPodder with correct,
to-the-point
strings and messages.
