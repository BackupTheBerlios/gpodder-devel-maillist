> Also, what do you think of adding a forum in addition to having the
> development mailing list. Do you think a forum would help users that
> are not familiar with mailing lists? I'm happy to read your opinion!

I'd generally vote in favor of a forum. As others have already pointed
out, I'd not go for the standard phpBB-style software.

As gPodder is all about usability and efficiency, I thought about
having kind of forum integrated within the wiki, so that users can
create a thread not in a general category but attached to a wiki page.
This should "force" the users to find the appropriate page and could
lead them to the correct answer during the search and thus keep a good
signal-to-noise ration ;)

I don't know if such software already exists (maybe MediaWiki with
LiquidThreads?), but this mail was mainly intended to get some
feedback about this idea.
