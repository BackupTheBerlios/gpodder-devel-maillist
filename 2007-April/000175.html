> If you're talking about simply marking an episode as played as soon as
> you click on the "Play" button, this is no problem. But I suppose you
> will want to mark it played only when the player application finished

> Another option would be to mark the episode played as soon as you click

The Podcast menu actually supports "Played/Unplayed" states. Its pretty
basic in that the moment you play the track on the ipod it marks it as
played in itunes. (as opposed to tracking that you finished listening to the
whole track).

In other words: Same principle as with "Hitting the play button" in the
player - just getting the information from the iPod.

This is exactly what I am referring to :-) "The blue dot". I am more than
