> I'm having problems with the length of some MP3s too. It could, of

Try installing the "python-pymad" package. If installed, gPodder will
prefer it over python-eyed3. PyMAD seems to be more correct for vbr MP3s
than eyeD3 (after all, eyeD3's main job is to handle tags, MAD's job is
to handle mp3 decoding).

If you installed pymad and sync to your iPod and newly copied episodes
still have the wrong length, please send a bug report to gpodder-devel.
