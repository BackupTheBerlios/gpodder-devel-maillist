> I finally managed to get gPodder to put podcasts on my iPod, but I
> still have a problem. I did a full sync, I played a podcast (from
> start to end using the forward button) and then I resync'ed.
> 
> Although the gPodder window says the episode has been played, gPodder 
> activated its "unplayed" mark on my iPod.

You have to sync your podcasts to your iPod again (even it you didn't
add any podcasts). Only then will gPodder read the metadata from the
iPod (and its local playback state database) and sync accordingly. It
should remove the "unplayed" mark then.

output from a "make test" synchronization session if it doesn't work
then.
