Hello!

I've just released gPodder 0.9.1 - about a month after the 0.9.0
release. This release includes everything that happened in our svn
repository during the last month.

The release notes can be found on the gPodder news page[1]. I've updated
the gPodder website to have a fresher look. I'm especially proud to
announce a new screenshots page[2] that should present gPodder's windows
and dialogs to first-time users. Please, have a look :)

[1] http://gpodder.berlios.de/news.html
[2] http://gpodder.berlios.de/screenshot-tour.html

Berlios mirror selection pages for the source and Debian packages:

http://prdownload.berlios.de/gpodder/gpodder-0.9.1.tar.gz
http://prdownload.berlios.de/gpodder/gpodder_0.9.1-1_all.deb

Thanks to all who helped make this release possible :)
