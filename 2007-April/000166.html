I am using KDE and Kaffeine as a media player. Gpodder only detects vlc and 
democracy player as a player on my pc, but I really would like to use 
Kaffeine.

It seems gpodder just looks in 
userappsdirs = 
[ '/usr/share/applications/', '/usr/local/share/applications/' ]

for the .desktop files. My kaffeine.desktop is located in 

/usr/share/applications/kde/kaffeine.desktop
(at least on Gentoo).

I just added /usr/share/applications/kde/ in the line, and now I can use 
Kaffeine and Amarok as media players.

Could you maybe implent this (if its the same on other distributions)?
