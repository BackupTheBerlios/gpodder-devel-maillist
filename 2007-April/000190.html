> I've implemented basic playback history support in the latest svn
> revision. Please try it out and tell me if it works for you. What you
> get:
>
> * Enable "Played" column in the Advanced tab of the Properties dialog
> * Playback files on your computer - the "Played" column will show a
>   green dot
> * Sync to your iPod - played back files shouldn't have a blue dot
>   anymore
> * Play back files on your iPod, then sync with gPodder - gPodder should
>   update the played back status of the podcasts accordingly
>
> This also works with older iPods (tested with iPod mini), and uses the
> playcount as a "played" indicator. Don't know if playcount increase
> means a fully played episode or just the start of the playback - test on
> your iPod.
