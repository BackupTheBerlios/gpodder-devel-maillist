The pauses after a download could be related to the tag updates. Just
run gPodder in the console with the --verbose switch (or "make test" if
you are using the SVN version) and after the download, if there is a
pause, the last line on the console should tell you that it's updating
the file's tag.

I don't know if I can make this faster, apart from making the tag update
happen just a second later after the download and putting it in its own
"tag update" thread to keep the GUI responding, or should I simply show
an information dialog that the tag is being updated?
