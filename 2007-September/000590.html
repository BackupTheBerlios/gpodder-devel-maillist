Hi all,

just my two cents. I have an iPod 5th gen (video), 30
GB, running Rockbox (www.rockbox.org). This works
perfectly fine with linux, because the player shows up
as a simple external hard disk. It also works fine
with gpodder. It is not necessary to repartition your
iPod, and in fact it becomes dual bootable, I can boot
mine with rockbox or the Apple firmware.

I chose rockbox just for fun, I've never used Apple's
firmware. The rockbox developers are lagging a bit
behind though, because they have to reverse engineer
most of the coding.

Anyway, just to let you, there's more than one way to
do it.

Wilfred
