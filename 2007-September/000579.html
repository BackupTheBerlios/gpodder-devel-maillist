On 12/09/2007, Thomas Perl <thp at perli.net> wrote:
>
> Hello, gPodder users and developers!
>
> We've had the new channel navigator since mid-July (that is, since
> 0.9.4). Since then, I've been thinking about removing the old ComboBox
> that lists the available channels, as this functionality is duplicated
> with the new channel navigator. Of course, it could be that somebody
> uses the combo box and has the channel navigator hidden. That's why I'm
> asking you to give some input and your opinion about this matter.
>
> Here's how it'd look like when removing the combobox:
>
> http://spock.thpinfo.com/~thp/images/gpodder-no-comboavailable.png
>
> It would bring some screen real estate (display ~ 2 more episodes in the
> available episodes list) and simplify the code a bit by removing the
> specific data fields only needed for the combobox.
>
> Do you think the combobox should be removed in favor of the channel
> navigator or can you tell me a good reason why the combobox should stay?



Looks fantastic.

My only concern would be  the resetting of the navigator after a download
has begun.

Currently, at least for me, if an episode is selected from a channel at the
end of the list, then once the download starts the navigator resets itself
back to the top of the list.

For people with small lists or very large screens this isn't a problem but
for me (who has, obsessively, 58 channels in the list) it can be annoying
that I have to keep scrolling through the navigator every time I download an
episode.
