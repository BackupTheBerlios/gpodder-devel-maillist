Hi Paul,

According to your 'dpkg -L python-gpod' output you are missing files, most 
importantly the gpod.py python module, which is confirmed when you try to do 
an 'import' of the non-existant module.

Try re-installing the python-gpod package, this should do the trick: 
sudo apt-get --reinstall install python-gpod


Hope it works,
