Hello gPodder users,

I've prepared deb and rpm packages for PyID3, I've tested them on Debian 4.0, 
Ubuntu 7.04 and Fedora 7. Here are the links:

deb --> http://nikosapi.org/gpodder/pyid3_0.5.5-1_i386.deb
rpm --> http://nikosapi.org/gpodder/PyID3-0.5.5-1.noarch.rpm
src --> http://nikosapi.org/gpodder/PyID3-0.5.5.tar.gz

In case you're wondering, PyID3 is used to extract the album art from 
individual podcast episodes. It is not a necessary dependency and is only 
worth it if you have an iPod with a color screen.

Cheers,

nick

P.S. Installing from source is also easy, just unpack and run ./setup install
