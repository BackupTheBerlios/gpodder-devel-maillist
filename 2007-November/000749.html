Hi Pierre,

We are aware of the fact that Ubuntu hasn't updated the version of gPodder
in its repositories. I opened a bug for that here:
https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gpodder/+bug/151193
I expect that gPodder will be updated (it has been updated to 0.10.0 in
Hardy, but that is just a sync with Debian unstable if I'm correct).

Regarding the md5'd directory names for each podcast, Thomas explained the
rationale behind that just a couple of days ago. Please see his e-mail:
https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/gpodder-devel/2007-October/001104.html
Maybe that situation can be changed. Patches are always welcome! :-)

Best regards,

Roel
