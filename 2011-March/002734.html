>> 1. I'm a little confused about the different branches --- as far as I
>> can tell, there are two "stable" branches (excluding tres) currently
>> being maintained: master and maemo-5 -- is that correct? What's the
>> relation between them? The patch applies cleanly against both of them;
>> but just so that I get a better understanding of the process: would it
>> be applied separately to both, or applied to one and then merged into
>> the other (and if so, in which direction)?
>
> The "master" branch is where the "stable" development happens. After
> every release, the "master" branch is tagged and the tagged commit is
> then merged into the "maemo-5" branch. Then, the Debian packaging is
> updated in the "maemo-5" branch, and a release is made (and it's also
> tagged with "fremantle/..."). So basically, "maemo-5" tracks "master"
> and just includes some Maemo-specific changes and the packaging info.
>

Thanks for the clarifications! I've added this information to the
