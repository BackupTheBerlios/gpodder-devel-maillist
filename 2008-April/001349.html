From: Harley Laue <losinggeneration at aim.com>

Attached is a patch (from svn) to change the Close menu item's label to Quit. 
This way it's more consistent with the toolbar button label. This is only a 
proposed fix. Changing the Quit button to Close would also be fine. This 
patch is just to add a bit of consistency.

As a side note, for me it tends to makes more sense to have Close 
hide/minimize to the tray and Quit immediately quit. Really though, the 
current behavior is fine. (OTOH, I think some people aren't used to the idea 
of Close minimizing to the tray. So this may actually be more confusing to 
these users.)
