> default? is transmission the default client for GNOME now?

How much are Torrent-based podcasts used nowadays? I personally would
also be happy to just discard the gnome-btdownload support and just
download/open .torrent files with the standard application - i.e. do no
special handling in gPodder itself, but support "opening" such files,
which in turn means that your default BitTorrent client will get to open
and use the file.

Is this a solution that is working for people? If so, I'd be happy to
strip out gnome-btdownload support in one of the future versions of
gPodder. Anybody has any use for the gnome-btdownload integration? If
so, please tell us!

Thanks for bringing this to our attention :)
