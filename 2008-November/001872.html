Here is a good compromise that I propose:

*) The menu item (and Ctrl+L) will _always_ open the URL entry dialog
*) The URL entry box will be made available as advanced config option
*) The URL entry box option will default to disabled/hidden

This way, even when the URL entry box is shown, we show the dialog,
eliminating the confusion of just focusing an already visible entry.

Any objections?
