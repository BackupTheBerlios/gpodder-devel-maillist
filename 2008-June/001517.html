On Sun, 2008-06-15 at 19:35 +0100, Paul Rudkin wrote:
> I am running SVN r743 and I notice when I run in verbose mode I get lots
> of the following messages:-
> 
> ﻿[  51.838] Looks like you have an old pubDate in your LocalDB
> [  51.868] Looks like you have an old pubDate in your LocalDB
> [  51.970] Looks like you have an old pubDate in your LocalDB
> [  52.027] Looks like you have an old pubDate in your LocalDB
> 
> Now they don't appear to do any harm though I did notice that one of my
> feeds lost all bar one of the episode entries (the files are intact).  I
> suspect this is because the older episodes are no longer in the feed?
> 
> What does the above message really mean and should I be concerned?  I
> have tried deleting all my .db files but the message still appears.

It just means it will have to convert the pubDate from the old format
(a RFC2822 string) to the new format (an unix timestamp).

It should mean no harm, and I just placed it there to notify users that
in case of problems with wrong pubdates, this could be the reason.

If it's working for you, you should not be concerned :)
