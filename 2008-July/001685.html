On Thu, 2008-07-24 at 18:55 -0400, Chris Arnold wrote:
> I am thinking about getting an iphone but i do not have a windows box
> so before i get one i want to make sure my fav podcast client works
> with the 3g 18gig iphone? I see where the touch somewhat works with

I can't say for sure, but if gtkpod / libgpod supports the iPhone,
gPodder should support it too. From what I know by reading on the
Internet, you definitely have to jailbreak your iPod touch/iPhone,
although I don't know if that still holds true nowadays.

If you want something "like" an iPhone, maybe you should think of a
Nokia Internet Tablet (N800, N810). They are not really phones, but you
can use Skype over WiFi, have a complete Linux environment in your
pocket and have many apps (BitTorrent, gPodder, MPlayer, games, ...)
running directly on your portable device. Yep - gPodder runs _directly_
on the Nokia N8x0.

For podcasts, I don't know if the iPhone maybe supports downloading
podcasts directly to the iPhone without the need for a computer...
